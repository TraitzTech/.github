## ✨ About Us  

**Traitz Tech** is a fast-growing startup based in **Bambili, Bamenda (North West, Cameroon)**.  
We focus on **SaaS, AI, Blockchain, Cloud, and IoT**, delivering **scalable, secure, and user-focused** digital products.  

💡 *Our name* “Traitz” reflects our foundation by **three pioneers** with different traits and strengths. Today, we’ve evolved into a team of **innovators, engineers, and dreamers** creating technology that transforms industries.
